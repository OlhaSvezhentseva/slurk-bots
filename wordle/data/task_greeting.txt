**Welcome to Wordle with Images**


In this game you will play classic wordle with a twist: not only you will also see an image related to 
the misterious word, but you will also play together with another person.

You will be able to enter you guess using the wordle keyboard on the right side of the web page,
but make sure to coordinate your guesses with your parnter, only if you enter the same word you will 
be able to advance in the game.

Have fun!
