**Welcome to Wordle with Images**


In this game you will play the classic wordle game, but with a twist: not only will you see an image related to
the mysterious word, but you will also play together with another person.

You will be able to enter you guess using the wordle keyboard on the right side of the window,
but make sure to coordinate your guesses with your partner: only if you enter the same word you will
be able to advance in the game.

Have fun!
